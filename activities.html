A ONE DAY SEMINAR: “ROLE OF WOMEN IN PROFESSIONAL LIFE”

We organized one day seminar on 11th February 2017. The goal of the seminar “Role of Women in Professional Life” is to bring high-potential women to build a foundation of leadership skills that will advance their personal as well as professional goals. Main focus of the seminar is to encourage women to explore their ideas and build professional network. The main objectives of the seminar are:

  1. To Develop Leadership Skills
  2. To Build Self Confidence
  3. Empowerment of Women in Profession
  4. Awareness About Women Rights in India
  5. Women’s Law in India
  6. Strategies for Effective Leadership at All Levels — in Every Situation

WORKSHOP : Machine Learning - An approach to achieve Artificial Intelligence and its applications

Department of Information Technology had organized this workshop on December, 18th-23rd 2017 under the coordination of Dr. Parth D. Shah, Ms. Sonal P. Rami Ms. Ayesha Shaikh & Mr. Ravi Patel. The main objective of this workshop is to investigate new solutions to scientific problems occurring in the various topics related to artificial intelligence applied in the domain of impaired people assistance. Financial support by ACM, GUJCOST and CHARUSAT



COMMUNITY SERVICE : Social Activities at Oldage Home at Anand Dham, lambhvel
