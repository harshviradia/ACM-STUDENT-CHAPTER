Who We Are

CHRUSAT is an official Student chapter of ACM initiated by Department of Information Technology, Chandubhai S Patel Institute of Technology. ACM student chapter was officially chartered by ACM's Chief Operating Officer on August 26, 2016, named as CHARUSAT ACM Student Chapter with Chapter Group ID:150102. The Chapter will serve students at CHARUSAT and other interested persons in the Anand community. DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The Department of Information Technology offers B.Tech. and PGDCS in Information Technology. The department has strong collaboration with industries and has signed MOU with many leading industries. The graduates have found remarkable placements in prestigious national and international companies and many are pursuing their higher studies from premier institutes in India and abroad. The department has competent and devoted faculty. Department was accredited for two year w.e.f 01/07/2016 by National Board of Accreditation.
