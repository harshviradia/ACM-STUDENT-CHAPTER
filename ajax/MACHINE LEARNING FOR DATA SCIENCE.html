Project Description

Machine Learning has become one of the mainstays of IT. ML includes various techniques that are used for achieving complex tasks associated with contemporary businesses. Its advantages include acquiring business intelligence, usage in Artificial Intelligence (Al) and Robotics, automation of tasks, resource utilization, contextual advertisements, intelligent recommendations, and quality assessment and so on. Even the most powerful method till date, "Growth Hacking" used machine learning as one of its strategy. Many classification models are viewed and detailed approaches to use these models are specified in this workshop. This is the rationale behind the proposal for conducting workshop on machine learning.
