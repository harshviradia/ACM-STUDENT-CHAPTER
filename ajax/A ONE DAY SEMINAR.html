Webinar Description

We organized one day seminar on 11th February 2017. The goal of the seminar “Role of Women in Professional Life” is to bring high-potential women to build a foundation of leadership skills that will advance their personal as well as professional goals. Main focus of the seminar is to encourage women to explore their ideas and build professional network. The main objectives of the seminar are:

  1. To Develop Leadership Skills
  2. To Build Self Confidence
  3. Empowerment of Women in Profession
  4. Awareness About Women Rights in India
  5. Women’s Law in India
  6. Strategies for Effective Leadership at All Levels — in Every Situation
